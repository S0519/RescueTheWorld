Our Services

Rescue world is an international organization that has proven instrumental in poverty elimination and disaster response in any part of the world. Our humanitarian activities are spread around the world as indicated in the following map. In countries like Mexico and El Salvador, for instance, rescue world supports education among children from unprivileged families by ensuring that each poor child has the necessary supplies to attend school. By providing the required education, the organization is guaranteeing that the children can build a better future that subsequently breaks their cycle of poverty within their respective community settings. In fact, aid programs in Mexico focus on supporting children from disadvantaged communities with school supplies while helping their families to begin economic activities that promise to eliminate their poverty in the long term. In another region, the Democratic Republic of Congo, rescue world is supporting children and pregnant women just like other non-governmental organizations featured by Lewis (2014) and Everill & Kaplan (2013). Barriers associated with healthcare provision and necessities for delivering babies with minimal mortality rates are being eliminated where rescue world is making contributions in terms of medical supplies. Rescue world is also supporting children who are orphaned with clothing, education and other needs that empower them to break their cycle of poverty in their future undertakings since their situation has been contributed majorly by prolonged war in the region. In Tanzania, rescue world is majoring on helping children and pregnant mothers in different perspectives according to their needs. Orphaned children are being provided with meals, accommodation, education, and healthcare services, especially in the rural areas. It has been noticed that in some regions, children study under tree shades thus limiting their learning capabilities. They have no blackboards, stationery, or food to sustain them in their learning environments, hence the need for assistance from Rescue The World. Additional activities are improving farming methods and supporting hospitals for effective maternal care and other general clinical services to enhance the healthy wellbeing of poor citizens. Texas has also benefited from the humanitarian activities of rescue world, especially in Fort Bend County. The floods destroyed property and left many people helpless without any means of resolving their situation. Through volunteering and member contributions, Rescue The World was able to collect and distribute clothing, household items, and other services that facilitated the victims recover to their normal lives. These are just examples of the humanitarian and development programs that the organization is undertaking under support from well-wishers from all over the world.
