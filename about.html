Mission statement

Rescue The World's mission is to provide humanitarian relief during disasters and to promote economic independence around the world. Rescue The World, sometimes referred to as the Entity or Organization, is a non-profit located in Richmond, TX. Our mission is to provide humanitarian relief during disasters and to promote economic independence around the world. Alleviate suffering and to preserve human life by providing humanitarian assistance to all those in urgent need around the world. To provide well-being and quality of life, save lives, alleviate suffering and provide comfort to those affected around the world. To eradicate poverty by supporting agriculture, education, health and community development priorities.

Where We Work
